About Us

Paramedic Ambulance

Equipped with trained paramedics and advanced medical equipment, these ambulances provide immediate assistance in critical situations.

Basic Life Support (BLS) Ambulance

BLS ambulances provide basic medical care for non-emergency situations, staffed with personnel to deliver initial medical aid.

Advanced Life Support (ALS) Ambulance

ALS ambulances are equipped for complex medical situations, staffed with trained personnel capable of advanced care.

Neonatal Ambulance

Specifically designed for transporting newborns, these ambulances are equipped with specialized equipment and trained professionals.

Emergency Medical Services

Rapid response units trained to handle emergencies, providing life-saving care at the scene.

Air Ambulance Services

Air ambulances for quick transportation to advanced medical facilities, especially in remote areas.
